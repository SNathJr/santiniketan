Visit the unique heritage and culture of Tagore Land

Shantiniketan, WB

Shantiniketan has the distinct identity of culture and heritage, which gives the place a unique soft touch. It's home to a number of local tribal communities, and The Visva Bharati university - an institution of national importance. Festivities, heritage sites and anual fairs are a significant part of the area's reputation.

The shightseeing tour in Shantiniketan comprises of visit to the famous Visva Bharati campus, the Ballavpur wildlife senctuary deer-park, Khoai mela. In Visva Bharati, 'the patha-bhavana' classrooms are under the skies and the unique university showcases the best of Indian traditation - the bests of semi-urban india and also highlights trditions from other cultures too.

Pack Accordingly

One of most important things when it comes to traveling is to packing accordingly. Here are a few tips:

  1. Light cotton clothing for summer
  2. Pack sunscreen
  3. Comfortable walking shoes
  4. Pack Mosquito Repellant
  5. Carry extra water just in case
  6. Woolens for winter
