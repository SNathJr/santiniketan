Santiniketan

Santiniketan has the distinct identity of culture and heritage, which gives the place a unique soft touch. It's home to a number of local tribal communities, and The Visva Bharati university - an institution of national importance. Festivities, heritage sites and anual fairs are a significant part of the area's reputation.

Check out the beauty of Santiniketan

The shightseeing tour in Santiniketan comprises of visit to the famous Visva Bharati campus, the Ballavpur wildlife senctuary deer-park, Khoai mela. In Visva Bharati, 'the patha-bhavana' classrooms are under the skies and the unique university showcases the best of Indian traditation - the bests of semi-urban india and highlights trditions from other cultures too.

Pack Accordingly

One of most important things when it comes to traveling is to packing accordingly. Here are a few tips:

  1. Light cotton clothing for summer
  2. Pack sunscreen
  3. Comfortable walking shoes
  4. Pack Mosquito Repellant
  5. Carry a flash-light
  6. Carry extra water just in case
  7. Woolens for winter
